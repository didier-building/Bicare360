## 🏥 The Problem We're Solving

```
REALITY IN RWANDA (AND AFRICA):

Hospital Patient
    ↓
Gets Treatment ← Costs: $1,000-5,000
    ↓
Discharged Home
    ↓
No Follow-up System
    ↓
Patients Stop Taking Medications
    ↓
Miss Appointments
    ↓
Complications Develop Silently
    ↓
Preventable Readmission
    ↓
Hospital Readmission Cost: $5,000+
Hospital Loses Money, Patient Suffers
```

**The Core Issue:**
- 🔴 No structured post-discharge care coordination
- 🔴 Patients don't know medication importance
- 🔴 No appointment reminders
- 🔴 Healthcare providers have zero visibility
- 🔴 Readmissions pile up unnecessarily

**Impact Numbers:**
- Rwanda: 50,000+ hospital discharges/year
- Current readmission rate: ~15-20% (many preventable)
- Cost per readmission: $5,000+
- Total wasted: $50M+ annually in Rwanda alone

## 🌟 Key Takeaways

### What Problems Does BiCare360 Solve?

1. **Post-Discharge Abandonment**
   - System ensures patients don't disappear after leaving hospital
   - Automatic daily reminders via SMS/WhatsApp
   - Nurse monitoring for critical issues

2. **Medication Non-Adherence**
   - Tracks every dose (scheduled vs. taken)
   - Sends SMS reminders at pill time
   - Alerts nurse if doses missed

3. **Missed Appointments**
   - Auto-creates appointment reminders (24h, 1h before)
   - If patient doesn't attend → Alert to nurse
   - Nurse reschedules at patient's convenience

4. **Nurse Overload**
   - System organizes patient queue by severity
   - Alerts prioritized by urgency
   - Can manage 10 patients vs. 3 previously

5. **No Outcome Data**
   - Every action logged (audit trail)
   - Medication adherence calculated daily
   - Readmission prevention tracked
   - Hospitals can prove ROI
